OUR STORY

Founded in Islamabad in 2023, the philosophy of SweetsHouse Bakeshop was simple; to create connections through desserts while striking the ideal balance between flavor and feeling. Since the beginning, SweetsHouse has secured an irreplaceable spot in the hearts of people. Being the quintessential representation of aesthetics and taste, a Layers dessert is consumed by the eyes well before delighting the tastebuds!

Reaching Nationwide

Growing & Expanding

he love for SweetsHouse has only blossomed with time. We now have 23 stores in different locations including Islamabad- and this is just the beginning. Our dream to bake the best desserts in town continues to flourish, and we are over the moon to have created a sweet utopia for dessert lovers to make their sweet escape from reality to fantasy!
